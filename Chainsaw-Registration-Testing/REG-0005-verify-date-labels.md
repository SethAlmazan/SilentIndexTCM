# REG-0005: Registration testing - Verify Registration Date and Expiry Date Labels

> Summary: Verify that the registration date and expiry date fields have clear labels in the chainsaw registration form.

**Preconditions:** The tester is logged in to Chainventory using a valid account and is on the chainsaw registration page.

**Scenario 1**

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Open the Chainventory website. | Verify that the system loads successfully. |
| 2 | Log in using a valid account, if needed. | Verify that the user is redirected to the dashboard. |
| 3 | Navigate to the `Registration` page. | Verify that the chainsaw registration form is displayed. |
| 4 | Locate the registration details section. | Verify that the section is visible and readable. |
| 5 | Check the `Registration Date` field. | Verify that the field has a clear `Registration Date` label. |
| 6 | Check the `Expiry Date` field. | Verify that the field has a clear `Expiry Date` label. |
| 7 | Select or enter valid dates in both fields. | Verify that the fields accept the selected or entered dates. |
| 8 | Review the form layout on desktop and mobile view, if applicable. | Verify that the date labels remain visible and readable. |

**Post-conditions:**

* Registration date and expiry date labels are displayed clearly.
* The date fields are understandable and usable by the tester.
