# REG-0002: Registration testing - Submit Valid Chainsaw Registration

> Summary: Verify that a user can submit a chainsaw registration form using valid and complete information.

**Preconditions:** The tester is logged in to Chainventory using a valid account and is on the chainsaw registration page.

**Scenario 1**

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Open the Chainventory website. | Verify that the system loads successfully. |
| 2 | Log in using a valid account, if needed. | Verify that the user is redirected to the dashboard. |
| 3 | Navigate to the `Registration` page. | Verify that the chainsaw registration form is displayed. |
| 4 | Fill out the owner information fields. | Verify that the fields accept the entered owner details. |
| 5 | Fill out the address and contact information fields. | Verify that the fields accept the entered address and contact details. |
| 6 | Fill out the chainsaw information fields. | Verify that the fields accept the entered chainsaw details. |
| 7 | Fill out the registration details fields. | Verify that the fields accept the entered registration details. |
| 8 | Upload required images, if applicable. | Verify that selected images are accepted by the form. |
| 9 | Click the `Submit` or `Register` button. | Verify that the registration form is submitted successfully. |
| 10 | Wait for the system response. | Verify that the system saves the registration record and redirects the user properly. |
| 11 | Go to the chainsaw records page. | Verify that the newly submitted registration appears in the records list. |

**Post-conditions:**

* A new chainsaw registration record is created.
* The submitted record is visible in the chainsaw records list.
