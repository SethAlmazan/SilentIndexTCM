# IMG-0004: Image upload testing - View Uploaded Images in Record Details

> Summary: Verify that uploaded proof of ownership and inspection images are displayed in the record details.

**Preconditions:** The tester is logged in to Chainventory using a valid account and at least one chainsaw record with uploaded images exists.

**Scenario 1**

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Open the Chainventory website. | Verify that the system loads successfully. |
| 2 | Log in using a valid account, if needed. | Verify that the user is redirected to the dashboard. |
| 3 | Click the `Records` navigation link or button. | Verify that the chainsaw records page opens. |
| 4 | Locate a record with uploaded images. | Verify that the record is visible in the records list. |
| 5 | Click the `View` or eye icon action for the selected record. | Verify that the record details modal or page opens. |
| 6 | Scroll to the proof of ownership images section. | Verify that uploaded proof of ownership images are displayed. |
| 7 | Scroll to the inspection images section. | Verify that uploaded inspection images are displayed. |
| 8 | Click an uploaded image, if clickable. | Verify that the image opens or can be viewed properly. |

**Post-conditions:**

* Uploaded images are displayed in the record details.
* Proof of ownership and inspection images are viewable by the user.
