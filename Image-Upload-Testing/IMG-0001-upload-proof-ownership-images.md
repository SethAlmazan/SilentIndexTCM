# IMG-0001: Image upload testing - Upload Proof of Ownership Images

> Summary: Verify that the user can upload proof of ownership images successfully.

**Preconditions:** The tester is logged in to Chainventory using a valid account and is on the chainsaw registration page.

**Scenario 1**

| # | Step | Expected Behavior |
|---|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Open the Chainventory website. | Verify that the system loads successfully. |
| 2 | Log in using a valid account, if needed. | Verify that the user is redirected to the dashboard. |
| 3 | Navigate to the `Registration` page. | Verify that the chainsaw registration form is displayed. |
| 4 | Locate the `Proof of Ownership` upload section. | Verify that the upload field is visible. |
| 5 | Select one or more valid image files. | Verify that the selected files are accepted. |
| 6 | Check the preview area. | Verify that the uploaded proof of ownership images are previewed properly. |
| 7 | Submit the registration form with valid information. | Verify that the registration is submitted successfully. |
| 8 | Open the submitted record details. | Verify that the uploaded proof of ownership images are displayed. |

**Post-conditions:**

* Proof of ownership images are uploaded successfully.
* Uploaded images are visible in the record details.
